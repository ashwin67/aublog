Solving the wrong problem

"What is the opposite of happiness? Sadness?  No.  Just as love and hate are two sides of the same coin, so are happiness and sadness.  Crying out of happiness is a perfect illustration of this.  The opposite of love is indifference, and the opposite of happiness is - here's the clincher - boredom." -Timothy Ferris, _ Four-Hour Workweek_ The point is that you may be solving a wrong problem
